# Upregulated lncRNA Survival Plots

This folder contains representative Kaplan–Meier survival plots for significantly upregulated prognostic lncRNAs in Kidney Renal Papillary Cell Carcinoma (KIRP).

The plots were obtained from the ENCORI Pan-Cancer platform after transcriptomic filtering and survival analysis. They are included as representative visualizations of candidate lncRNAs identified in this study.

## Included lncRNAs

- AL590666.2
- BCAN-AS2
- CYTOR
- LINC00958
- SNHG6
- ZFAS1
